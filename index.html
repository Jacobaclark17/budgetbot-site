BudgetBot

A personal budgeting and debt-payoff app — connect your free cash flow to an optimized payoff plan.

What it does

BudgetBot takes your monthly cash flow and routes it into an optimal debt-payoff plan — showing your debt-free date, interest saved, and net worth over time. It grew out of a spreadsheet that tracked savings and debt on separate tabs and never connected them. Connecting them is the product.

Read-only Data never sold No money movement

How it uses Plaid

With your permission, BudgetBot uses Plaid to securely read your account transactions and balances so it can show your spending, net worth, and payoff plan against real data.

  • Read-only. BudgetBot can never move money, make payments, or initiate transfers.
  • Your credentials stay with your bank. Bank logins are entered through Plaid — BudgetBot never sees them.
  • You stay in control. Link and unlink any time from within the app or Plaid Portal.

Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-07-07 · Contact: jacobaclark17@gmail.com

The short version

Your financial data stays on your device. BudgetBot never sells, shares, or monetizes your data — the product is a paid app, not your data.

What we process

Data you enter (budget, deductions, debts, an optional manually-entered credit score) and, only if you choose to link an account, read-only transactions and balances retrieved via Plaid.

Where it lives

All processing is local to your device. Plaid-retrieved transactions and balances are held in memory during a session and are not written to disk unencrypted. Any bank access token is stored encrypted under your operating-system user account.

What we never do

  • No sale, sharing, or transfer of your data to third parties.
  • No advertising, no lead generation to lenders, no data-derived revenue.
  • No payments or money movement — the Plaid scope is read-only.

Consent

Linking an account is initiated by you through Plaid Link, which presents Plaid's own consent flow. You can withdraw consent at any time by unlinking.

Retention & deletion

Data is retained only on your device and only until you delete it — via in-app reset, clearing the feed, or revoking the Plaid connection (which severs bank access and invalidates the token). This policy is reviewed periodically for compliance with applicable privacy law.
